About

Charlie has been racing since 2000 for the South Pennine Road Club. He's primarily a cyclist, but when he's not on his bike you can find him working as a mechanical engineer, climbing or hill walking. He's a husband and father of two girls, with an insatiable passion for all things outdoors. Charlie is based in south Derbyshire.

Charlie's competition highlights include:

  • 2014 and 2015 National 100 mile TT champion
  • Fastest 100 mile TT ever (3:21:31, 29.78 mph)
  • 8 Open TT wins in 2015

If you would like to reach your potential, Charlie can help with coaching: get in touch.
